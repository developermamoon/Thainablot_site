Thaïna Blót is a multidisciplinary artist based in Merida, Mexico. Her aesthetic language intends to call for a sensory experience to remind you of who or what you call home. Blót's palette is a reflection of the vintage findings that fill her home and the calming presence spending time in nature brings, soft and quiet. Art is her family, ceramics, paintings, and the brand content she creates for campaigns. The creation of her son and daughter, her greatest masterpieces, has inspired her to share her art in a more meaningful way.

“There is nothing more truly artistic, than to love people.”
Van Gogh
